### Project Objective

This ChatGPT project aims to assist users in drafting, revising, and perfecting medical school personal statements. The goal is to create statements that demonstrate key qualities sought by admissions committees: motivation, reflection, growth, authenticity, and alignment with the medical profession's values.

---

### Key Features of the ChatGPT Personal Statement Project

1. **Rubric Integration:**
    
    - Integrate the scoring rubric directly into the project, allowing users to evaluate their drafts within the chat.
        
    - Guide users to self-assess based on Content, Structure, Style, and Impact.
        
2. **Guided Revision Prompts:**
    
    - Include contextual prompts that help users refine specific parts of their statement.
        
    - Suggested prompts:
        
        - "Make my introduction more compelling."
            
        - "Enhance the reflection in this paragraph."
            
        - "Give feedback on my closing statement’s impact."
            
3. **Simulated Adcom Skimming:**
    
    - Incorporate a feature that evaluates the first and last sentences of paragraphs, simulating how busy adcom members might skim the text.
        
    - Prompt examples:
        
        - "Check if my opening and closing sentences are strong and memorable."
            
        - "Do my first and last sentences create a cohesive narrative?"
            
4. **Tone and Authenticity Checks:**
    
    - Enable users to test whether their tone feels natural and sincere.
        
    - Example prompt: "Does my statement sound authentic and relatable?"
        
5. **Refinement Suggestions:**
    
    - Offer recommendations to eliminate clichés, overly formal language, and vague statements.
        
    - Example prompt: "Identify parts that sound cliché or too formal."
        
6. **Grammar and Clarity Support:**
    
    - Automate proofreading for grammar and sentence structure improvements.
        
    - Example prompt: "Proofread this paragraph for clarity and correctness."
        

---

### Workflow Integration

1. **Draft Submission:**
    
    - Users submit their draft to ChatGPT for initial feedback.
        
2. **Rubric Scoring:**
    
    - ChatGPT guides the user in scoring their draft using the integrated rubric.
        
3. **Targeted Revisions:**
    
    - Users specify which parts of the draft they want to improve.
        
    - ChatGPT offers targeted advice based on rubric scores and user input.
        
4. **Iteration and Feedback:**
    
    - ChatGPT tracks changes and improvements across iterations.
        
    - Users receive a summary of how revisions enhanced their statement’s quality.
        
5. **Final Review:**
    
    - ChatGPT performs a holistic review, focusing on tone, structure, and content alignment with the rubric.
        
6. **Output:**
    
    - A polished draft that aligns with medical school expectations, highlighting personal growth, motivation, and professional readiness.
        

---

### Success Metrics

- High rubric scores in Content, Structure, Style, and Impact.
    
- Strong, engaging first and last sentences in each paragraph.
    
- Consistent tone and authentic voice throughout the statement.
    
- Positive, constructive feedback from peers or mentors.
    

By embedding these structured, guided features into the ChatGPT project, users will have a reliable and supportive tool to craft standout medical school personal statements.
