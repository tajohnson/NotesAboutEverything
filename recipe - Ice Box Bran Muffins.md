Ice Box Bran Muffins
Judy Johnson


2c kellogs Bran Buds

1c boiling water

Pour boiling water over bran buds, let sit 1 hour

Cream 
	1 1/2 c sugar
	1/2 c shortening
	
Add:	
	2 eggs, beaten
	2c buttermilk
	2 1/2 c flour
	2 1/2 tsp baking soda
	1 c nabisco bran (not available any more - use Kellogg's All Bran "Original"
	1/2 c dates or nuts (optional)
	1/2 tsp salt.
	
Fold in soaked bran mixture
	
	Can be kept in refrigerator for up to 6 weeks if covered.
	
	Fill muffin tins 2/3 full.
	
	Bake at 375 for 30-35 minutes.
